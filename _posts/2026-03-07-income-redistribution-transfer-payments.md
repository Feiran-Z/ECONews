**Original Article:** [Shrinking Welfare in the United States Will Bring Political and Social Consequences](https://carnegieendowment.org/posts/2026/01/shrinking-welfare-benefits-united-states)

## News Summary

The Carnegie Endowment article examines the political and social consequences of shrinking welfare benefits in the United States, focusing on how changes to social safety net programs affect income redistribution and economic stability. The analysis highlights how welfare programs like SNAP and SSI have evolved, with recent trends showing benefit reductions and stricter eligibility requirements. The article discusses the economic implications of these changes, including potential impacts on poverty rates, income inequality, and social mobility. It explores the political dynamics driving welfare reform debates and how different ideological perspectives shape policy approaches to income redistribution through transfer payments.
