# Blog Post Structure Template

## Required Format (from website-requirements.md)
```
# [YYYY-MM-DD] News Source: News Title

**Source:** News Source
**Original Article:** [URL]
**Date:** Month Day, Year

## Summary

[One paragraph, maximum 300 words. Summarize the main economic issue, event, and/or policy discussed in the article.]

## IB Economics Connections

[One paragraph, maximum 300 words. Suggest relevant IB economics theories and frameworks to analyze and evaluate the main economic event/policy/issue presented in the article.]

## Tags
[At most 5 tags featuring the most relevant economic topics or theories]
```

## Educational Tone Guidelines
- Write for IB Economics students (ages 16-19)
- Explain economic concepts clearly but not simplistically
- Connect news to specific syllabus topics
- Use diagrams conceptually (describe them in text)
- Include real-world examples from the article

## Common Analysis Approaches
1. **Market failure analysis**: Identify negative/positive externalities, public goods, common pool resources
2. **Government intervention evaluation**: Assess effectiveness of taxes, subsidies, regulations
3. **International trade implications**: Analyze tariffs, quotas, trade agreements
4. **Macroeconomic impacts**: Consider effects on inflation, unemployment, growth
5. **Distributional effects**: Examine equity vs. efficiency trade-offs

## Tag Selection
- Choose 2-3 most relevant economic concepts
- Use standard economic terminology
- Examples: Pigouvian Taxes, Market Failure, International Trade, Fiscal Policy, Monetary Policy
