## Selection Criteria Applied

### 1. Syllabus Relevance (40% weighting)
- **BBC article**: Direct connection to market failure (negative externalities), Pigouvian taxes, government intervention - **Score: 38/40**
- **Reuters India article**: Focuses on trade aspects (CBAM) - relevant but narrower scope - **Score: 32/40**
- **CNN global shipping article**: International dimension but less direct to core IB topics - **Score: 28/40**
- **Other articles**: Good but older or more specialized - **Score: 25-30/40**

### 2. Clarity & Accessibility (25% weighting)
- **BBC article**: Clear, concise, accessible language for students - **Score: 23/25**
- **Reuters articles**: More technical, assumes some trade knowledge - **Score: 18/25**
- **CNN article**: Good but focuses on political dimension - **Score: 20/25**

### 3. Detail & Depth (25% weighting)
- **BBC article**: Specific examples, stakeholder perspectives, implementation challenges - **Score: 22/25**
- **Reuters India article**: Detailed but specialized on steel industry - **Score: 20/25**
- **Other articles**: Varying levels of detail - **Score: 15-20/25**

### 4. Timeliness (10% weighting)
- **BBC article**: February 2026 (very recent) - **Score: 10/10**
- **Reuters India article**: February 2026 - **Score: 10/10**
- **Other articles**: 2024-2025 (older) - **Score: 5-8/10**

## Final Selection: BBC Article
**Total Weighted Score: 93/100**

### Why this article is best for IB Economics students:
1. **Clear market failure example**: Ferry emissions as negative externality
2. **Multiple economic concepts**: Pigouvian tax, elasticity, equity-efficiency trade-off, government failure
3. **Real-world implementation challenges**: Infrastructure limitations, regional disparities
4. **Accessible case study**: Concrete example students can visualize and analyze
5. **Recent and relevant**: Current policy implementation with ongoing debate

### Alternative articles considered:
- **Reuters India article**: Strong for international trade unit but less comprehensive for core microeconomics
- **CNN global shipping article**: Good for global dimension but more political than economic analysis
- **Denmark farm tax**: Innovative but very specialized (agricultural emissions)

The BBC article provides the most balanced and comprehensive foundation for teaching multiple IB Economics concepts through a single, accessible case study.
