Focus on comprehensive coverage:
- Balance across units based on remaining topics
- Consider current economic events
- Ensure conceptual connections between weeks

### Long-term (Weeks 25+)

- Revisit high-importance topics
- Cover emerging economic issues
- Address gaps in understanding

## Notes and Adjustments

### Topic Difficulty Assessment
- **Easy to find news**: Monetary policy, trade, inflation, unemployment
- **Moderate difficulty**: Market failures, government intervention, supply-side policies
- **Challenging**: Economic theory, methodology, abstract concepts

### Seasonal Adjustments
- **Exam periods** (May, November): Focus on core concepts
- **Budget seasons**: Focus on fiscal policy
- **Economic summits**: Focus on global economy topics
- **Data release periods**: Focus on economic measurement

### Geographic Considerations
- Ensure global economic coverage
- Balance developed vs. developing economy examples
- Include diverse geographic regions
- Consider local economic contexts where relevant
