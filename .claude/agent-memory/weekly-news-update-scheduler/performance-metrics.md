# Performance Metrics

## Overview
Tracking system performance to ensure weekly news updates meet quality standards and educational goals.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### 1. Success Rate
**Definition**: Percentage of weeks with successful blog post creation
**Target**: >90%
**Calculation**: (Successful weeks / Total weeks attempted) × 100

### 2. Coverage Rate
**Definition**: Percentage of IB Economics syllabus topics covered
**Target**: 100% over 31+ weeks
**Calculation**: (Topics covered / Total topics) × 100

### 3. Quality Score
**Definition**: Adherence to website-requirements.md standards
**Target**: 100% compliance
**Components**:
- Frontmatter completeness: 25%
- Content structure: 25%
- Educational value: 25%
- Language and tone: 25%

### 4. Timeliness
**Definition**: Posts created on schedule (Sunday completion)
**Target**: 100% on-time completion
**Measurement**: Completion within 24 hours of scheduled time

### 5. Diversity Score
**Definition**: Balanced coverage across economic concepts and regions
**Target**: Even distribution across 4 syllabus units
**Components**:
- Unit balance: 40%
- Geographic diversity: 30%
- Conceptual variety: 30%

## Quality Assessment Rubric

### Frontmatter (25 points)
- [ ] Layout: "post" (5 points)
- [ ] Title format: "[YYYY-MM-DD] News Source: News Title" (5 points)
- [ ] Date: Correct YYYY-MM-DD format (5 points)
- [ ] Author: "ECONews Team" (5 points)
- [ ] Original URL: Valid, working link (5 points)
- [ ] Tags: 1-3 relevant economic topics (5 points)

### Content Structure (25 points)
- [ ] Original Article link with title (5 points)
- [ ] News Summary section (max 300 words) (5 points)
- [ ] IB Economics Analysis section (max 300 words) (5 points)
- [ ] Tags section with 1-3 tags (5 points)
- [ ] Proper markdown formatting (5 points)

### Educational Value (25 points)
- [ ] Clear IB syllabus connections (10 points)
- [ ] Appropriate for IB students (16-19) (5 points)
- [ ] Academic but accessible language (5 points)
- [ ] Professional tone (5 points)

### Language and Tone (25 points)
- [ ] Grammar and spelling (10 points)
- [ ] Clarity of explanation (5 points)
- [ ] Engagement level (5 points)
- [ ] Consistency with ECONews style (5 points)

**Total Score**: 0-100 points
**Quality Rating**:
- 90-100: Excellent
- 80-89: Good
- 70-79: Satisfactory
- 60-69: Needs improvement
- Below 60: Unacceptable

## Error Analysis

### Error Categories and Impact

| Error Type | Frequency | Impact | Resolution Time |
|------------|-----------|--------|-----------------|
| Search Failure | Expected: 10% | Medium | 1-2 hours |
| Quality Failure | Expected: 5% | Low | 30 minutes |
| Technical Failure | Expected: 2% | High | 2-4 hours |
| Formatting Failure | Expected: 3% | Low | 15 minutes |

### Root Cause Analysis
1. **Search Failures**: Insuitable articles, source limitations, topic specificity
2. **Quality Failures**: Article depth, educational value, source credibility
3. **Technical Failures**: API limits, network issues, agent errors
4. **Formatting Failures**: Template deviations, markdown errors, frontmatter issues
